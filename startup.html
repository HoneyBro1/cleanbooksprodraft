For more than a decade, the technology industry has been booming around the world. And among other things, technological innovations have spawned a new generation of start-ups with new business models so different from traditional companies that they require entirely new management structures to be successful. Hence the need for a Virtual Accountant.

CleanBooks Pro is a corporate finance experts' firm that offers Virtual Accounting Services to Start-Ups or Businesses, including expertise, and advice on a project-by-project basis. We concentrate on the financial side of your business!

Subject matter areas commonly covered by our Virtual Accounting services include:

  • Accounting & Bookkeeping
  • Budgeting& Cash Flow Management
  • Internal Audit and setting up SOPs
  • Tax Return preparation

Our services are flexible and offer start-ups an affordable way to employ a qualified financial expert. One of the benefits of using our service is cost savings. The fees paid by start-ups or SMEs are substantially lower than what they would have to pay if they hired a full-time traditional corporate accountant.

We offer numerous benefits as Virtual Accountant, and many start-ups require these services for some of the following reasons:

First of all, we ensure that you do not need to spend so much money to hire in-house accountants and set up infrastructure like offices and IT networks to manage invoices and accounts. Instead, you can focus on running your core business activities and growing your business exponentially rather than having to worry about these administrative tasks.

Second, with CleanBooks Pro, we ensure that you have access to all the information needed to run your business (as long as it is in electronic form) because there is no physical limitation such as having a separate accounting firm for accounts payable or another just for payroll, etc. These give you a lot more flexibility in running your business.
